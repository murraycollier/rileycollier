Riley Collier

Musician. Creative. Dreamer.

Hello, I'm Riley Collier, a musician deeply rooted in the world of folk guitar. My sound is a tapestry woven from the influences of artists like Noah Kahan and the ethereal vibes of Pink Floyd. Through my music, I aim to capture the essence of storytelling and emotion, blending heartfelt lyrics with intricate melodies. Each song is a journey, reflecting my experiences and the world around me. I invite you to join me on this musical adventure, where every strum of the guitar resonates with a piece of my soul.
